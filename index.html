About

“Jazz washes away the dust of everyday life.” — Art Blakey

Formed in Palo Alto, Magic Jazz Band unites Stanford-area musicians with a shared love for jazz. Featuring instrumental and vocal tunes, blending swing, latin, and modern favorites, the band’s lineup shifts from intimate duos to lively full ensembles. They’ve played community concerts, block parties, and private events, offering an authentic acoustic jazz experience that connects. Always live, always warm, always a little magic.
